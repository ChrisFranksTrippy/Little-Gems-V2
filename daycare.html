Little Gems Daycare

At Little Gems, we have a safe and enclosed outdoor space for dogs, complete with sand pits, ball pits, tunnels and plenty of toys to keep your dog occupied. Inside we have nice big space for more playing with toys and chews as well as sofas and dog beds for when they need a rest!

£14 PER DAY

(Over 5 Months old)

£20 PER DAY FOR PUPPIES

(Puppies from 11 weeks old or fully vacinated)

Puppy price includes toilet training and basic training

We can only accept dogs that have been fully vaccinated (including Kennel Cough) neutered/spayed (this does not apply to dogs under 6 months old) be well socialised and friendly.

All play is supervised.

We are perfect for puppies!

Little Gems warmly welcomes puppies to our doggy day care!

Because we only accept friendly socialised dogs, your puppy will learn from the best! They will join the pack and will learn appropriate doggy behavior from the older dogs.

This will teach your pup, respect and essential doggy manners, which will be very important to your puppy, as they grow older.

Your puppy will never be left alone at Little Gems.

All play is supervised and puppies will have essential rest periods throughout the day.

Our puppy day care price includes toilet training and basic training: sit, stay, lead work etc. This price will reduce to £14 a day once your puppy is 5 months old.
